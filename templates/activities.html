✨ What It Can Do

Here's what you can use your AI assistant for

📋 Getting Started ✨ What It Can Do 📝 Cheat Sheet ❓ FAQ

💻 Programming & Coding

Get help writing code, fixing bugs, or learning new programming languages.

Try asking:
"Help me write a Python program to organize my files"
"What's wrong with this code?" (then paste your code)
"Teach me the basics of JavaScript"

🔍 Research & Information

Search the web, find information, summarize articles, or learn about any topic.

Try asking:
"Find information about climate change"
"Summarize this article: [paste URL]"
"What are the best productivity tips?"

📝 Writing & Editing

Write emails, letters, essays, or edit your writing for clarity.

Try asking:
"Help me write a professional email"
"Proofread this and suggest improvements" (paste your text)
"Write a birthday card for my friend"

🛠️ Tech Troubleshooting

Get help with computer problems, software issues, or tech questions.

Try asking:
"My computer is running slow, what can I do?"
"How do I install Python on my computer?"
"Explain what a virus is in simple terms"

📅 Organization & Planning

Help with to-do lists, scheduling, organizing your thoughts.

Try asking:
"Help me plan a week of meals"
"Create a study schedule for me"
"What should I focus on today?"

💡 Problem Solving

Brainstorm ideas, think through decisions, or get a fresh perspective.

Try asking:
"I need ideas for my small business"
"Help me think through this decision..."
"What are some creative gift ideas?"
