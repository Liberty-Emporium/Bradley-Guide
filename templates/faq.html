❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about using your AI assistant

📋 Getting Started ✨ What It Can Do 📝 Cheat Sheet ❓ FAQ

Is it free?

It depends on the plan you choose. Check kilo.ai for current pricing.

Is it safe?

Yes! Your AI assistant runs locally on your machine or within your secure account. It doesn't share your data with strangers. Just don't share passwords or personal secrets with it, and you'll be safe.

Can it do X?

Probably! The AI is very capable. If it can't do something, it'll tell you. Just ask and see what happens!

What if I ask something wrong?

No problem! Just rephrase your question. The AI will do its best to understand what you mean.

Does it remember our conversation?

It remembers within the same conversation, but not across different sessions. Just tell it context if you need to refer back to something.

Can I use it for work?

Yes! It can help with emails, documents, research, coding, and much more. It's great for productivity.

What if it gives wrong information?

AI can sometimes make mistakes. Double-check important information. If something seems wrong, ask it to clarify or research it yourself.

How do I get help if stuck?

Ask Jay! Or check the Kilo documentation at docs.openclaw.ai
